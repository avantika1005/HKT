**🎓 ESKIMO – Student Risk Dashboard**

🚨 AI Early Warning System to Prevent School Dropouts


### 📌 Overview

The **Student Risk Dashboard** is an AI-powered early warning system developed to help schools identify students who may be at risk of leaving school prematurely. By analyzing key indicators such as attendance patterns, academic performance, mid-day meal participation, distance from school, and family background, the platform generates a **dropout risk score** while highlighting the most important factors influencing that risk. This enables teachers and school administrators to recognize warning signs at an early stage, implement timely support measures, and track student progress over time, ultimately helping prevent disengagement and reduce dropout rates.

--- 

### ✨ Features

* 📊 **Risk Prediction Model** – Predicts student dropout risk and explains the **top contributing factors**.
* 🛠 **Intervention Playbook** – Suggests **evidence-based actions** such as counselling sessions, home visits, and peer mentoring.
* 📩 **Parent Communication Generator** – Creates **respectful and supportive messages** for parents without using stigmatizing language.
* 🎯 **Government Scheme Matcher** – Identifies **relevant scholarships and welfare schemes** students may be eligible for.
* 🗺 **District Risk Heatmap** – Visualizes schools with **higher concentrations of dropout risk** to support district-level decision making.
* 📈 **Intervention Outcome Tracker** – Monitors **attendance and academic trends after interventions** to measure improvement.
